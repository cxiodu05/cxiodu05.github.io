HARDCORE TANO*C

由众多日本电子音乐制作人一起创立的独立音乐团体。本团体最初由永井良和（REDALiCE/赤いひと）创立，当时本社团以ヾ(゜Д゜)ノハードコアタノシー名义活动，但在2007年在发布专辑《HARDCORE SYNDROME》后将社团名称改为“HARDCORE TANO*C”。 TANO*C即“楽しい”的谐音，意为快乐、开心。

Team Grimoire

Team Grimoire made his first appearance in BEMANI as one of the winners of the KAC2012 Original Song Contest for SOUND VOLTEX BOOTH. While he is known for his baroque style of hardcore, he has also touched on a number of other genres, having a particular affinity towards gabber, crossbreed, and mainstream hardcore. His popularity started to pick up after his first SOUND VOLTEX appearance and soon after, he found himself appearing in a handful of doujin releases.
